## Original V2 Issue

- Existing v2 captured only four HUIM top-level categories.
- It omitted all method leaves, HAUIM/HURM/HUSRM/HUSPM/HUEM, and Advanced
  Topics.

## Uncertainties

- Orange `extend` arrows from HUIM to HAUIM/HURM are relation edges, not
  ordinary parent-child edges. This draft preserves HAUIM/HURM as UPM-level
  branches and notes the extension relation as a visual relation.
- `Advanced Topics` is visually attached below the main taxonomy by a downward
  arrow; this draft treats it as a UPM-level branch.
